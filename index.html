What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive Health & Safety solutions tailored to your business. We make compliance straightforward, practical, and built to last.

01

Fire Risk Assessments

A legal requirement for all non-domestic premises in the UK. Our thorough, site-specific assessments go beyond the checklist — we identify vulnerabilities, evaluate your current controls, and deliver a clear, prioritised action plan you can act on immediately.

  • Full premises survey & risk identification
  • Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order compliance
  • Written report with prioritised action plan
  • Follow-up review & ongoing support
Request an Assessment
Most Popular
02

Health & Safety Audits

A complete health check of your workplace safety management system. We examine your policies, procedures, documentation, and physical environment to give you a holistic view of where you stand — and a clear roadmap to where you need to be.

  • Comprehensive workplace walkthrough & interview
  • Gap analysis against HSE guidelines
  • Policy & documentation review
  • Detailed audit report with RAG rating
Book an Audit
03

FFE Training

Practical, engaging Fire Fighting Equipment training delivered on-site. Your team won't just understand the theory — they'll have the hands-on confidence to respond calmly and correctly in an emergency, making your workplace genuinely safer.

  • Theory & practical hands-on training
  • Extinguisher types, use, and limitations
  • Emergency evacuation procedures
  • CPD-recognised certification on completion
